Built for Combat

Designed specifically for the rigorous demands of combat robotics, the Magnum X-2200 eliminates common failure points found in standard hobby motors. The can is reinforced with a 1mm steel ring to prevent deformation from impacts.

The magnets are glued with high-temp epoxy and retained by a kevlar wrap to ensure they stay in place even under extreme shock loads.

Key Features

  • Reinforced steel flux ring for impact resistance
  • N52SH High-temperature magnets (150°C rating)
  • 0.2mm silicon steel stator laminations for high efficiency
  • Hardened 8mm Titanium shaft with flat spots
  • Quadruple bearing support system

Technical Specifications

The Magnum X-2200 is engineered to provide maximum torque density. With a 380KV rating, it is optimized for 6S-12S LiPo setups, delivering up to 4.5kW of peak power.

All motors are dynamic balanced to within 0.05g to ensure smooth operation at high RPMs.

Shipping & Warranty

Ships with bullet connectors pre-soldered. Includes mounting hardware pack.

Backed by our 1-year combat warranty. If it breaks during normal match use (excluding direct weapon hits to the can), we'll replace it.
